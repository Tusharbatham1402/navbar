Hydroponic Farming


Hydroponic farming is soil-less cultivation where plants grow with water, nutrients, controlled systems, technology, efficiency, sustainability, and high yield.


Types of Hydroponic Farming


Main hydroponic types are Deep Water Culture, Nutrient Film Technique, Ebb and Flow, Wick, Drip, and Aeroponics systems.


Benefits of Hydroponic Farming


Hydroponic farming saves water, uses less space, grows faster, yields more, reduces pests, and enables sustainable year-round cultivation.
